Capsule C1

PROJECT & ROLE

Bridge any distance with Capsule C1 - the device that turns your TV into a lifelike calling experience. Feel as if family and friends are right there with you, no matter how far apart you are.

I designed the complete Capsule C1 product experience, including hardware and software interfaces, distribution strategy, and all marketing materials and website assets. The technical development involved 3D printing and testing over 20 casing prototypes to achieve an optimal design—compact, well-designed, with proper thermal management for the Raspberry Pi. I focused on perfecting the core user experience: optimizing call sound quality, picture clarity, and color accuracy for any display. I handled software development including RTP protocols and HDMI CEC setup, added custom sound effects to the mobile and TV applications, and directed and edited the product demonstration video.
